ABOUT

Who we are?

Crepixa is a Digital Product Design Agency, that comes from two words, namely Creative and Pixel. The idea of this name is driven from our beliefs about the important impact, that small pixels can create in art works. Crepixa has a team of professional and multidisciplinary experts and offers several services and solutions not only in the field of Graphic Designs, but also in Web Development and Mobile Apps as well.

think to create

Vision:

Crepixa aims to strengthen its position in the fields of Graphic Designs, Motion Graphics, Web Development and Marketing through recruiting professionals and also through offering a high quality services and solutions to our customers. Our uniqueness in Web Development lay on the combination between the beautiful Designs and robust Codes, so that your customers can enjoy visiting and surfing your website comfortably.
